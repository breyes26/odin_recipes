Chimol

A healthy, fresh and delicious salsa that utilizes the wonderful radish

This is a honduran recipe that is traditionally served with carne asada, but it also goes great with fish, tofu or chicken. It even is wonderful as a dip for tortilla chips or veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup radish, chopped in medium fine diced
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped in medium fine dice
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients and enjoy!
